Fortune High School

Fortune High School is a public school where character embodies discipline, excellence and good taste. It fulfills its mission and advocacy of empowering the youth for a brighter tomorrow by honing the students academically while enhancing their potential by continuously developing their talents and skills and instilling good manners and proper conduct that would strengthen their values and faith in GOD.

DEPED VISION, MISSION AND OBJECTIVES
DepEd VISION

 

We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose values and competencies enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation. As a learner-centered public institution, the Department of Education continuously improves itself to better serve its stakeholders.

DepEd MISSION

 

To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where:
STUDENTS learn in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment.
TEACHERS facilitate learning and constantly nature every learner.
ADMINISTRATORS AND STAFF, as stewards of the institution, ensure an enabling and supportive environment for effective learning to happen.

FAMILY, COMMUNITY, AND OTHER STAKEHOLDERS are actively engaged and share responsibility for developing life-long learners.  

VISION

 

“A premier school where character embodies discipline, excellence and good taste in a fast changing environment.”

MISSION

 

Fortune High School aims to provide quality basic education and develop learners into well-rounded individuals with discipline, excellence and good taste capable of coping with the challenges of modern technology and demands of society.

GOALS:

Readiness for the full implementation of the Senior High School technical-vocational courses attuned to the interest and aptitude of the students. The program intends to provide skills that can lead to productive employment after graduation.

To provide effective teaching interventions, appropriate learning programs and activities that will enhance learning outcomes such as improved NAT results.

To give adequate and conducive teaching-learning environment to teachers and students.

To strengthen and sustain partnerships among stakeholders for a collaborative support in implementing DepEd thrusts and programs.

To secure teacher’s effectiveness by providing them equal opportunities for professional growth and allowing them academic freedom. 

CORE VALUES

>Excellence
To achieve on both ends what is significant technically and morally upright. The realization of achieving more than what’s achievable in order to be in proximity with perfection academically and to be of great service and productivity to the community.

>Discipline
Discipline is the assertion of willpower over more base desires, and is usually understood to be synonymous with self-control. Self-discipline is to some extent a substitute for motivation, when one uses reason to determine the best course of action that opposes one’s desires.

>Good Taste
Good taste is an individual’s personal and cultural patterns of choice and preference. It is drawing distinctions between things such as styles, manners, consumer goods and works of art and relating to these. Social inquiry of taste is about the human ability to judge what is beautiful, good and proper.
