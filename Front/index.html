FORTUNE HIGH SCHOOL

Fortune High School is a public school where character embodies discipline, excellence and good taste. It fulfills its mission and advocacy of empowering the youth for a brighter tomorrow by honing the students academically while enhancing their potential by continuously developing their talents and skills and instilling good manners and proper conduct that would strengthen their values and faith in GOD.
